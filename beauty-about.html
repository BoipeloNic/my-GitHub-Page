About Boi's Beauty Bar

The Founder

Meet Boipelo Nicole

Hi, I'm Boipelo Nicole Ditshwenyego — a certified makeup artist based in Gaborone, Botswana, and the founder of Boi's Beauty Bar.

My passion for makeup started as a love for art — the face as a canvas, beauty as an expression. What began as a personal obsession quickly grew into a calling. I trained, I practiced, I perfected, and now I get to do what I love every single day.

Whether it's a soft natural beat or a full dramatic glam, I believe every client deserves to feel like the most beautiful version of themselves. At Boi's Beauty Bar, you're not just getting makeup — you're getting confidence.
