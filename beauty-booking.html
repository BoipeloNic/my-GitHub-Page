Booking Policies

💳 Deposit Policy

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking. Your appointment is only confirmed once the deposit has been received.

❌ Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. Late cancellations will forfeit the deposit. No-shows will be charged the full service fee for future bookings.

⏰ Late Arrival Policy

Please arrive on time for your appointment. A grace period of 15 minutes is allowed. Arrivals beyond 15 minutes may result in a shortened service or rescheduling at full cost.

📞 Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation via WhatsApp or email within 24 hours of submitting your request. Payment details for the deposit will be shared at that time.
